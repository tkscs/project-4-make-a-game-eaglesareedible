I want to create a snake game with the ambitious goal of adding in animations.
– Menu and reset screen
Small pieces
– Movement
– eating the apple and spawning a new one
– Having customizable levels. 

Ambitious goal:
theme:
    Like in the normal snake game, a snake grows by eating an apple, but if it crashes into its own long body, or the walls, the poor snake will become dizzy and confused, and the level will end. 
Interactions:
    The player will be able to move the snake in different directions, and it will continue moving in whichever direction the player presses. You can move in long straight lines, or pull off intricate and quick manuevers. When they reach an apple, the player will eat the apple, making it dissapear, and another apple will appear somewhere else. 
How to win the game:
    The game is won if the player eats all the apples in one level without dying. 

Animations: One animation would be that an apple would expand and contract in place. 

The levels I am thinking of are similar to in the snake game on google. e.g. randomly spawning walls as another obstacle, the apples moving as well, changing the size of the game area, changing player speed, and having more apples spawn.
